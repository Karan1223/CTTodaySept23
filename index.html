SANY India delivers 8 Units of SCC7500A, one of the biggest Crawler Cranes in India, to Sanghvi Movers Limited

SANY India has delivered 8 units of their 750-ton crawler crane, the SANY SCC7500A, to Sanghvi Movers Limited (SML) in the first quarter of the 2023-24 financial year, increasing SML's fleet to a total of 10 units. This reinforces SML's position as India's largest Hoisting Solutions Company with a fleet of 45 SANY cranes for core infrastructure projects. The formal handover ceremony, attended by senior executives from both companies, took place at The Ritz Carlton in Pune, marking their continued partnership in advancing India's infrastructure growth.
